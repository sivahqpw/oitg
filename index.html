The Goodies Glossary

Before you dive in, here's a cheat sheet:

  1. Opt-In: When someone willingly signs up to get emails from you.
  2. Email Marketing: Sending emails to promote products, services, or content.
  3. Subscriber: Someone on your list who said, "Yes, please send me your stuff!"
  4. Niche: Your passion or the topic you're all about.
  5. Affiliate Marketing: Promoting other people's stuff for a commission.
  6. Conversion: When a subscriber takes a desired action, like buying something.
  7. List Segmentation: Dividing your list into smaller groups for targeted emails.
  8. Autoresponder: Automated emails sent at set intervals.
  9. Open Rate: The percentage of folks who open your email.
  10. Click-Through Rate (CTR): The percentage who click on links in your email.
